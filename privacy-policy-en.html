Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 13, 2024

We at TikTok Auto Scheduler are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register and use our services, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • TikTok account information (username, no passwords)
  • Payment information (processed by secure third parties)
  • Customer support and contact information

1.2 Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the application:

  • Data about uploaded and scheduled videos
  • App usage time and frequency
  • Device and operating system information
  • IP address and connection information

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze app usage
  • Provide relevant content

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide services: Execute scheduling and automatic video posting functions
  • Improve services: Analyze and optimize app performance
  • Customer support: Answer questions and resolve issues
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Legal: Comply with legal requirements and regulations
  • Marketing: Send product and service notifications (with consent only)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information in the following cases:

3.1 With Service Partners

  • TikTok (to perform video posting functions)
  • Payment service providers
  • Cloud and storage service providers
  • Analytics service providers

3.2 Legal Requirements

When required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety and those of users
  • Investigate illegal activities

4. Information Security

We implement strict security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Authentication: Use OAuth 2.0 and standard security protocols
  • Access Control: Limit data access to authorized personnel only
  • Monitoring: Continuously monitor suspicious activities
  • Backup: Regular encrypted data backups

5. Data Retention

We store your information for:

  • As long as your account remains active
  • As necessary to provide services
  • As required by law
  • To resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After account deletion, personal data will be deleted within 30 days, unless law requires longer retention.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Rectification: Update inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of personal information
  • Restriction: Restrict information processing
  • Portability: Request data transfer to another provider
  • Objection: Object to information processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: privacy@tiktokscheduler.com

7. Cookies and Preferences

You can control cookies through:

  • Web browser settings
  • Our cookie management tools
  • Opt-out options in the app

Note that disabling cookies may affect app functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that:

  • These countries have equivalent data protection levels
  • We use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses
  • We comply with international data transfer regulations

9. Children

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If discovered, we will delete such information immediately.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Important changes will be notified through:

  • Email to registered addresses
  • In-app notifications
  • Website announcements

Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tiktokscheduler.com
  • Address: 123 Main Street, City, State 12345, USA
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567

We are committed to responding to all requests within 30 business days.

12. Legal Compliance

This policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable international regulations

This policy is effective from September 13, 2024.
